## Steps

1. Submit your trip details and $105 refundable deposit.
2. Pixie concierges match you with a verified owner and confirm availability.
3. Sign the intermediary agreement and pay the remaining balance directly to the owner.

Need to tweak dates? Reply to the concierge email and we will adjust your request without restarting the queue.

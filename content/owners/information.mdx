## What to Expect

1. Submit your resort details, use year, and availability windows.
2. Pixie concierges match bookings that align with your point charts.
3. We draft the intermediary agreement and capture the guest deposit.
4. Funds arrive within 5 business days after check-out (net of platform fees).

## Owner Concierge Perks

- Verified guests with identity and payment checks.
- Automated reminders for banking windows and point strategy.
- Dedicated payouts dashboard with downloadable statements.

Need help? Email **owners@pixiedvc.com** and our concierge team will reach out in under 24 hours.

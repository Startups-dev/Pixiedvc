## Key Clauses

- **Point Stewardship** – Owners confirm they control the points required for the stay.
- **Guest Assurance** – PixieDVC screens guest payments and communicates confirmed reservation numbers.
- **Timeline** – Owners supply confirmations within 24 hours; PixieDVC releases payouts after checkout.
- **Cancellations** – Deposits are refunded to guests if no match occurs by the SLA; PixieDVC covers change fees when we adjust itineraries.

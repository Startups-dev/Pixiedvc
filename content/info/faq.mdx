## Can I book without creating an account?

Yes! Submit a trip request and we will help you create credentials once we are close to matching.

## How long does matching take?

Most stays match within 3 to 7 days depending on season and villa type.

## Are deposits really refundable?

Absolutely. If we cannot match you inside the SLA you select, we automatically refund the deposit.

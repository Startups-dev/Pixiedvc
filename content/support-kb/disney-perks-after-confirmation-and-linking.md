Once a PixieDVC reservation is confirmed, guests receive a Disney confirmation number.

That confirmation can be linked in My Disney Experience.

After linking, guests can typically:
- view reservation details in the Disney app
- manage park planning tools
- make dining reservations through Disney systems
- use Disney transportation available for their resort location
- access Early Theme Park Entry where available
- use Lightning Lane planning features based on Disney's current policies

Feature availability can vary by resort, park day, and Disney policy updates.

If reservation-linking details are missing for a confirmed booking, concierge support can help with next steps.

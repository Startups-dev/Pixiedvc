PixieDVC reservations follow structured cancellation policies because Disney Vacation Club reservations are tied to member points.

This means policies can differ from traditional hotel cancellation rules.

Outcomes depend on timing relative to check-in. Depending on timing, a cancellation may qualify for a refund or for a deferred cancellation credit toward future travel.

For reservation-specific outcomes, review the booking terms shown in your agreement and confirmation details.

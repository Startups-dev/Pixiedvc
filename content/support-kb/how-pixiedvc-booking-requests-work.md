A booking request starts with your dates, resort preferences, party details, and stay needs.

After submission, PixieDVC reviews the request and works to match it with verified owner availability.

If a match is available, you are guided through confirmation and payment steps.

If availability changes, concierge can help you review nearby alternatives, date options, or room adjustments.

Request-specific timelines can vary by season, resort demand, and room category.

A typical PixieDVC reservation follows a structured sequence from planning to confirmation.

Most guests start in one of three places: a resort page, the trip calculator, or a Ready Stay listing.

Step 1: Explore resorts and trip options.

Step 2: Use the calculator for an estimate. The estimate is not a confirmed reservation.

Step 3: Submit a stay request with dates, resort preference, room type, and guest details.

Step 4: PixieDVC checks availability with DVC owner points for the requested stay.

Step 5: If availability is found, reservation details are prepared for guest review.

Step 6: Guests review and accept agreement terms, then complete required payment steps.

Step 7: Reservation confirmation is issued after agreement and payment requirements are completed.

If an exact request cannot be matched, guests can usually review alternatives such as nearby resorts, different room types, or adjusted dates.

Ready Stays can move faster than custom requests because the reservation opportunity is already pre-confirmed.

Payment timing depends on how far in advance the reservation is made.

The $99 availability deposit is separate from this schedule and is used to begin owner matching.

## When do I pay for my reservation?

Payment happens when the booking agreement is accepted and signed.

If the trip is more than 90 days before check-in:
- 70% payment at booking
- 30% due no later than 90 days before check-in

If the trip is within 90 days of check-in:
- 100% payment at booking

If booking is more than 90 days before check-in, payment is typically split:
- 70% due at booking
- 30% due no later than 90 days before check-in

If booking is within 90 days of check-in, full payment is typically required at booking.

Payment is completed during the stage where the reservation agreement is accepted and signed.

If you are reviewing a specific booking screen, follow the displayed timing and terms for that reservation.

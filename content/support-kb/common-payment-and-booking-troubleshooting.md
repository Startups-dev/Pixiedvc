If payment fails, first confirm card details, billing information, and available funds, then retry once.

If checkout appears stuck, refresh the page, sign back in, and return to the active booking step.

If the issue continues, avoid repeated retries and contact concierge support with a short summary of what happened.

Include the page you were on and any visible error message so support can help faster.

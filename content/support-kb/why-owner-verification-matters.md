Owner verification supports trust and consistency in the booking ecosystem.

It helps reduce risk, improve match quality, and keep guest expectations aligned with owner eligibility.

For guests, this adds confidence that listings come through a structured process.

For owners, it supports safer and clearer participation in the platform workflow.

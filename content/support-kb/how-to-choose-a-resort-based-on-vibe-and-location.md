Choosing a DVC resort is easiest when you pick by vibe first, then transportation/location.

For a lively or walkable EPCOT-area feel, guests often shortlist BoardWalk or Beach Club.

For easier Magic Kingdom access, Bay Lake Tower, Grand Floridian Villas, and Polynesian Villas are common top picks.

For a calmer atmosphere, guests frequently consider Saratoga Springs, Old Key West, or Kidani Village.

For a refined modern feel, Riviera is a frequent choice; for tropical atmosphere and classic appeal, Polynesian is often preferred.

A good concierge-style first pass is to shortlist 2-4 resorts that match your vibe, then refine by room type, transportation priorities, and trip pace.

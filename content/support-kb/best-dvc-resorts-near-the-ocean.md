For ocean-adjacent DVC stays, the strongest options are Aulani, Disney's Hilton Head Island Resort, and Disney's Vero Beach Resort.

Aulani is best when you want a destination-style Hawaii trip with a full resort atmosphere.

Hilton Head is often preferred for a calmer coastal pace and a quieter vacation feel.

Vero Beach is a good fit when you want a straightforward beachfront-style Disney stay with a laid-back rhythm.

If your priorities are relaxation, travel time, or family activity level, concierge can help you narrow the best match.

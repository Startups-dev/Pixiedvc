PixieDVC reservations are not standard hotel inventory.

Disney Vacation Club stays are created with member points, so availability has to be verified before a reservation can be confirmed.

When a guest submits a stay request, PixieDVC starts an owner-matching process to find a DVC member who can create that reservation for the requested resort, room type, dates, and stay length.

Availability can change quickly based on demand, resort popularity, room type, and point inventory.

A request becomes confirmed after availability is successfully matched and the booking moves through agreement and payment steps.

If a request cannot be matched, PixieDVC can suggest alternatives such as nearby resorts, different room types, or adjusted travel dates.

The $99 availability deposit starts this matching and verification process. It is refundable until an owner match is secured and becomes non-refundable after a match is secured.

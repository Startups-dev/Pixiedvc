Trip timing can affect crowd levels, weather, and reservation availability.

Common quieter periods (historically):
- mid-January to early February
- late April to early May
- early September

Common peak periods:
- Christmas and New Year
- Spring Break weeks
- summer months
- major holiday weekends

Weather patterns in Orlando:
- summer: hot, humid, and frequent afternoon storms
- winter: generally mild
- spring/fall: often comfortable temperatures

Resort planning by park priority:
- Magic Kingdom focus: Bay Lake Tower, Polynesian Villas, Grand Floridian Villas
- EPCOT/Hollywood Studios focus: Beach Club Villas, BoardWalk Villas, Riviera Resort

Resort planning by travel style:
- families: Beach Club Villas, Polynesian Villas, Animal Kingdom Villas at Kidani Village
- couples: Grand Floridian Villas, Riviera Resort, Copper Creek Villas

Transportation can also shape resort fit:
- monorail-area convenience: Bay Lake Tower, Polynesian Villas, Grand Floridian Villas
- Skyliner convenience: Riviera Resort

If availability is limited, flexibility with dates, room type, or resort selection usually improves booking success.

After selecting a Ready Stay, guests move through the standard PixieDVC booking sequence:

1. Review the stay details.
2. Accept the reservation agreement.
3. Complete payment according to the payment schedule.
4. Reservation moves toward confirmation.

Ready Stays can move faster because the stay is already structured.

Ready Stays are still tied to live Disney Vacation Club availability and are not guaranteed until booking is completed.

A Ready Stay may disappear if another guest secures it, availability changes, or the reservation opportunity is no longer valid.

If a stay disappears, guests can review similar alternatives or submit a custom request for more flexibility.

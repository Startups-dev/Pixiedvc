For Disney Vacation Club reservations, Dining Plans are not typically added directly by the guest in My Disney Experience.

Instead, the DVC owner can usually request/add the Dining Plan on the guest's behalf through Disney Vacation Club Member Services.

PixieDVC can coordinate this request between the guest and the owner.

In many cases, adding the Dining Plan may require valid payment information so the owner can complete the purchase.

PixieDVC does not charge a service fee for coordinating a Dining Plan request.

Because Disney policies can change, final availability and process details may vary by reservation and timing.

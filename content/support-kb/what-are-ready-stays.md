Ready Stays are prebuilt stay opportunities surfaced on PixieDVC for faster booking decisions.

They are designed for guests who want a quicker path than a fully custom request.

Each Ready Stay still has specific dates, room details, and booking terms you should review before checkout.

If you need custom dates or different room options, a standard request may be a better fit.

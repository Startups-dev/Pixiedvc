Ready Stays are pre-assembled reservation opportunities with specific stay details already defined.

Each Ready Stay is structured around a specific:
- resort
- room type
- travel dates
- length of stay

Because the reservation structure already exists, Ready Stays can often move faster than a custom stay request.

Ready Stays are not guaranteed until booking is completed. Availability can change if another guest secures the stay first.

If you need custom dates or different room options, a standard request may be a better fit.

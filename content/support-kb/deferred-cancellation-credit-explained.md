Deferred Cancellation Credit allows the value of a canceled reservation to be applied toward a future stay.

This option may apply when a direct refund is not available under cancellation timing and reservation policy terms.

Cancellation outcomes depend on timing relative to check-in and the reservation agreement accepted during booking.

Earlier cancellations may allow more flexibility, while later cancellations are usually more restricted.

For reservation-specific outcomes, guests should review their agreement terms and confirmation details.

If this is your first DVC stay, choose a resort that keeps transportation simple and the vibe unmistakably Disney.

## Great first-time picks
- **Bay Lake Tower**: Walkable to Magic Kingdom and easy to navigate.
- **Beach Club Villas**: Epcot access plus a fun pool complex.
- **Riviera Resort**: Modern design and Skyliner convenience.

## How to choose
Start with which park you plan to visit most. Then consider whether you want a calm retreat or a lively resort atmosphere.

## Next step
Use the calculator to compare a few resorts side by side and pick the stay that fits your dates and budget.

# Why Disney Vacation Club Resorts Are Considered Deluxe

## Introduction
Guests often hear that Disney Vacation Club resorts are Deluxe resorts, but may not know what that really means.

This guide explains what sets DVC resorts apart and why they are classified at the Deluxe level.

## Location and Park Proximity
Many DVC resorts are located close to theme parks.

Shorter travel times, walking paths, and convenient transportation contribute to a more comfortable experience.

## Room Size and Layout
DVC rooms are generally larger than standard hotel rooms.

Many include kitchen features, separate living areas, and layouts designed for longer stays.

## Resort Amenities
Deluxe resorts offer enhanced amenities.

These often include:

1. Multiple dining options
2. Feature pools and recreation areas
3. Well designed common spaces

These amenities are part of what defines the Deluxe category.

## Overall Experience
The Deluxe designation reflects a combination of comfort, location, and service.

For many families, this creates a more relaxed and immersive vacation.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are all DVC resorts Deluxe
Yes. All DVC resorts fall into Disney's Deluxe resort category.

### Do Deluxe resorts cost more
Cash prices are higher, which is why renting DVC points can offer strong value.

# What Documents You Should Keep for a DVC Rental

## Introduction
Keeping the right documents organized helps guests feel confident throughout the DVC rental process. While most stays go smoothly, having information readily available reduces stress.

This guide explains which documents are important and why.

## Reservation Confirmation
The Disney confirmation number is the most important item.

It confirms that the stay exists in Disney's system and allows verification if needed.

## Rental Agreement
The rental agreement outlines terms and responsibilities.

Guests should keep a copy accessible before and during the trip.

## Payment Records
Proof of payment provides clarity and protection.

This may include receipts or transaction confirmations.

## Communication Records
Keeping emails or messages related to the booking can be helpful.

Clear communication history supports quick resolution if questions arise.

## How to Store Documents
Guests may store documents digitally or in print.

What matters most is easy access when needed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to bring printed documents
Digital access is usually sufficient, but printed backups can add reassurance.

### Should I share documents with travel companions
Yes. Sharing key details helps everyone stay informed.

## Next Step
With documents organized, guests can focus on enjoying their Disney vacation with confidence.

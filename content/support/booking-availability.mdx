  - question: "Can I check DVC availability on Disney.com?"
    answer: "No. DVC availability is managed in the member system, not on Disney.com. We check availability through owner access."
  - question: "How far in advance should I book?"
    answer: "Home resort bookings can open up to 11 months in advance. Other resorts typically open at 7 months."

Availability is tied to DVC member booking windows and can change quickly. We confirm availability through verified owner access and keep you informed if we need flexibility.

## Purpose
We retain data only as long as needed for operations, legal obligations, and customer service.

## Scope
This policy applies to operational data, audit logs, and financial records stored by PixieDVC.

## Responsibilities
- Leadership approves retention standards.
- Engineering implements retention and deletion workflows.

## Policy statements
- Operational data is retained while accounts are active and as required for service delivery.
- Financial records are retained according to legal and accounting requirements.
- Audit logs are retained for compliance and security review.
- Data deletion requests are honored where applicable and permitted by law.

## Review cadence
This policy is reviewed at least annually and whenever a material change occurs.

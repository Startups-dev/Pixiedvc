## Purpose
We protect customer, partner, and internal data by using secure systems, limiting access, and monitoring critical activity.

## Scope
This policy applies to PixieDVC employees, contractors, and any systems that store or process PixieDVC data.

## Responsibilities
- Leadership owns this policy and approves updates.
- Engineering maintains secure configuration and access controls.
- All team members follow least privilege and report issues quickly.

## Policy statements
- We use least-privilege access for all systems and data.
- Data in transit is protected by TLS.
- Data at rest is protected by our infrastructure providers.
- Admin activity is recorded in admin audit events.
- We review security-relevant changes before release where practical.

## Review cadence
This policy is reviewed at least annually and whenever a material change occurs.

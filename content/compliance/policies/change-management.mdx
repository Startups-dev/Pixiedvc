## Purpose
We manage changes to reduce risk and keep production stable.

## Scope
This policy applies to changes in PixieDVC production systems, code, and configuration.

## Responsibilities
- Engineering prepares and deploys changes.
- Leadership reviews high-risk changes and approves releases.
- All changes are tracked in version control.

## Policy statements
- Changes are tracked in git and released through a defined workflow.
- Production releases are recorded in the admin change log.
- Emergency changes are allowed but must be documented after the fact.
- Rollback plans are considered for production changes.

## Review cadence
This policy is reviewed at least annually and whenever a material change occurs.

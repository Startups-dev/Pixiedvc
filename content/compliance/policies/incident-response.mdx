## Purpose
We respond quickly to security and availability incidents to reduce impact and prevent recurrence.

## Scope
This policy applies to incidents affecting PixieDVC systems, data, or services.

## Responsibilities
- Leadership coordinates response and communication.
- Engineering investigates, mitigates, and documents the incident.
- All team members report suspicious activity or outages immediately.

## Policy statements
- Incidents are logged in the admin compliance incident log.
- We prioritize containment, then recovery, then root cause analysis.
- We document timelines, impact, and corrective actions.
- We perform a post-incident review for material incidents.

## Review cadence
This policy is reviewed at least annually and whenever a material change occurs.

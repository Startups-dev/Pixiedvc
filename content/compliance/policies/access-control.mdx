## Purpose
We ensure only authorized people can access PixieDVC systems and data.

## Scope
This policy applies to all PixieDVC applications, infrastructure, and vendor systems.

## Responsibilities
- Leadership approves admin access.
- Engineering enforces access controls and maintains admin gating.
- Team members use unique accounts and do not share credentials.

## Policy statements
- Access is granted based on role and business need.
- Admin access is restricted to approved staff only.
- Access is removed promptly when roles change or access is no longer required.
- Admin routes and sensitive data are restricted to authorized users.
- Periodic access reviews are performed and recorded manually.

## Review cadence
This policy is reviewed at least annually and whenever a material change occurs.
